about me

Daily knoweledge booster (www.dailyknowledgebooster.in) is a leading website providing valuable, convenient, relevant and enjoyable online education pertaining to GK & Current Affairs to students, aspirants and general public through a diversity of services. This website is a venture of Nitesh kumar. The main orientation of this website is to create a large repository of knowledge for the general reference of students and aspirants. Daily booster was launched in august, 2018 and is a pioneer in providing high quality content to students and aspirants of various examinations in India. The 2 member team of Daily knowlegde booster includes former aspirants of the Civil Services Examination.
